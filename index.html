Ansoff Matrix The Ansoff Matrix is a strategic planning tool that provides a framework to help executives, senior managers, and marketers devise strategies for future growth. It is named after Russian American Igor Ansoff, an applied mathematician and business manager, who created the concept. The matrix shows four strategies that can be used to help a firm grow and also analyzes the risk associated with each strategy.
